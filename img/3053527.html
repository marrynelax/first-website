    What are the benefits of a Premium Membership?

    Answer

    As a Premium user you will be able to download licenses that will allow you to use all those resources included in Freepik Selection without attribution. Furthermore, your download limit will be extended from 30 downloads per day to 100. 

    As a Premium user you will also have access to our new Premium content, which are exclusive resources created for you!

  • Question

    How many resources can I download per day?

    Answer

    Non-registered user:

    As a user who is not registered in the website, you can make up to 5 downloads per day.

     

    Registered user:

    As a free user, you have a download limit of 30 downloads per day.

    Remember that if you download the same file more than once during the same day it will count as one file downloaded. However, if you download a file 

    The download limit will be restored at 23:59:59 GMT +02.

    If you desire to extend this limit you must acquire any of our subscription plans. You can check our membership offers on the following link:

     https://profile.freepik.com/premium
     

    Premium user:

    If you become a premium user, you will have a limit of 100 downloads per day.

    We wish to allow our users to download a high number of resources. However, to avoid users not making a responsible usage of the downloads we have stated this limit. The downloads allowed are enough for most of our users but we understand that there might be users who may need to download more resources for specific purposes that are in accordance with our terms of use. If you are in this situation, please send us an email to support@freepik.com and we will let you know if you meet the requirements for this.

    Please note that the download limit will be restored at 23:59:59 GMT +02.

  • Question

    Is my subscription plan automatically renewed?

    Answer

    Yes. Your subscription plan will renew automatically unless you cancel this option (no discounts apply for renewals). To cancel the automatic renovation of your plan and avoid future non-desired payments you must cancel your subscription from your user's profile.
    Please make sure you do this after you have received a message from Freepik confirming that the payment of your purchase has been successfully completed, otherwise you will cancel your subscription completely.

  • Question

    Can I cancel my plan and order a refund of my purchase?

    Answer

    Yes. Once your Premium Membership has been activated you will have a 15 day period to order a refund of your payment. You may request your refund contacting us through support@freepik.com

    What is the difference between the Free and Premium accounts?

    Answer

    Free account: You must add a link to Freepik and credit the author in exchange for using the graphics. To credit the author, you must include the attribution line Designed by Freepik in case the graphics were made by Freepik, or the author's name in other case. Read more
    Premium account: You can use the graphics without crediting the author and you can use it as many times as you wish. Also you will have access to some graphics which are exclusively available for Premium users. Read more
